Privacy Policy for Sabbai Ko Kitchen

**Effective Date: November 28, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how Sabbai Ko Kitchen ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, post recipes, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, username, and password.

We also automatically collect certain information when you access or use our Services, including IP address, browser type, operating system, and data about your usage patterns (like pages visited).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services and content (recipes).
  • To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.
  • To communicate with you about your account and provide technical support.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to provide the Services (e.g., hosting providers) or when required by law. We do not sell your data.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@sabbaikokitchen.com.
